# Todo Serverless Scaleway

## Preparation

### Accounts

*** RULE 1 ***

Apply MultiFactor Authentication (MFA) on your main account

*** RULE 2 ***

Don't use your main account for daily business or POC's like this. It is easier to delete an "WORKER" account when its credentials are compromised.

*** RULE 3 ***

Don't commit productive/cloud accounts, keys or passwords.
